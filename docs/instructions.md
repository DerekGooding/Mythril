# How to Play Mythril 

Mythril is a job-based incremental RPG where you manage a party of characters, unlock powerful abilities (Cadences), and tactically manage stats through Junctioning.

## 🕹️ Core Gameplay Loop

### Characters & Stats
You control multiple characters, each with four primary stats:
- **Strength (STR)**: Influences gathering and combat task duration.
- **Vitality (VIT)**: Influences physical endurance and survival tasks.
- **Magic (MAG)**: Influences research and refinement speed.
- **Speed (SPD)**: Influences exploration and movement tasks.

### Quests & Tasks
- **Starting Tasks**: Drag a Quest card from the **Locations** tab onto a character to start it.
- **Task Types**: 
  - **Single**: One-time story quests that unlock new content.
  - **Recurring**: Repeatable tasks for farming resources.
  - **Unlock**: Special tasks that grant permanent access to new systems or locations.
- **Duration**: High stats relevant to a task will reduce its completion time.

### The Workshop (Refinement)
Once you unlock refinement abilities, you can use the **Workshop** to turn raw materials (like Iron Ore or Moonberries) into refined magic (like Fire I or Life I). These are essential for Junctioning.

### Junctioning Magic
Junctioning allows you to assign refined magic to your character's stats for significant bonuses.
- **Tactile Junctioning**: Drag magic items directly from your **Inventory** onto a character's stat badge (e.g., S for Strength).
- **Stat Preview**: While dragging, you will see a preview of how the stat will change (↑ for increase, ↓ for decrease).
- **Removal**: Use the "Link Off" icon on the character display to enter Removal Mode, then click any junctioned stat to clear it.
- **Limits**: Stats are capped at a maximum of **255**.

### Cadence Completion
Similarly, you can track your research progress for each job in the **Cadence** tab. The counter shows how many abilities you have unlocked out of the total available for that Cadence. A green checkmark indicates you have mastered that job!

### The Journal
The **Journal** tab provides a historical log of your party's recent activities.
- **Task Tracking**: It records the last 50 completed quests, refinements, and cadence unlocks.
- **Details**: Each entry shows which character completed the task, what was achieved (e.g., rewards gained), and the exact time of completion.
- **Persistence**: Your journal history is saved automatically and persists across sessions.

## 💡 Tips for Success
- **Specialization**: Differentiate your characters! Give one high Magic for fast research and another high Strength for resource farming.
- **Task Sorting**: In the **Locations** tab, use the "Sort by Duration" toggle to quickly find short-burst tasks for active play or long-duration quests for idling.
- **Automation**: Once you unlock "Auto-Quest" abilities, you can toggle automatic task looping for characters. This only affects the **primary (first) task slot**. Quests in additional slots must be restarted manually.

### Magic Capacity
- **Capacity**: Watch your magic limits. Research "Magic Pocket" abilities to increase the amount of magic you can carry.
