# Guidance Request: Character Progression Model

**Date:** 2026-03-03
**Agent:** Gemini CLI

## Context
Currently, character stats (Strength, Vitality, Magic, Speed) are primarily derived from assigned Cadences and Junctioned magic. There is no concept of "Character Level" or permanent stat growth that stays with the character regardless of equipment.

Options:
1.  **Pure Gear/Job Model**: Stats are 100% determined by what is equipped. This keeps the focus on tactical management.
2.  **Hybrid Model**: Characters earn XP from quests and level up, gaining permanent base stat increases. Equipment then acts as a multiplier.
3.  **Mastery Model**: Mastering a Cadence (unlocking all abilities) gives a small permanent stat boost to that character.

## Questions
1. Should characters have their own leveling system, or should we stick to the equipment-driven model?
2. If we add permanent growth, should it come from general XP or specific "Job Mastery"?
