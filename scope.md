# Game Scope

## Core Mechanics

*   **Turn-Based Combat:** Classic turn-based combat system.
*   **Materia System:** Equipable orbs that grant abilities, spells, and stat boosts, based on Final Fantasy VII.
*   **Job System:** Characters can change jobs, which determines their stats, abilities, and available equipment, based on Final Fantasy Tactics.

## Materia System (Final Fantasy VII)

### Magic Materia (Green)

*   **Barrier:** Casts Barrier, MBarrier, Reflect, and Wall.
*   **Comet:** Casts Comet and Comet2.
*   **Contain:** Casts Freeze, Break, Tornado, and Flare.
*   **Destruct:** Casts DeBarrier, DeSpell, and Death.
*   **Earth:** Casts Quake, Quake2, and Quake3.
*   **Exit:** Casts Escape and Remove.
*   **Fire:** Casts Fire, Fire2, and Fire3.
*   **Full Cure:** Casts Full Cure.
*   **Gravity:** Casts Demi, Demi2, and Demi3.
*   **Heal:** Casts Poisona, Esuna, and Resist.
*   **Ice:** Casts Ice, Ice2, and Ice3.
*   **Lightning:** Casts Bolt, Bolt2, and Bolt3.
*   **Mystify:** Casts Confu and Berserk.
*   **Poison:** Casts Bio, Bio2, and Bio3.
*   **Restore:** Casts Cure, Cure2, Cure3, Regen, and Life.
*   **Revive:** Casts Life and Life2.
*   **Seal:** Casts Sleep and Silence.
*   **Shield:** Casts Shield and Shield2.
*   **Time:** Casts Haste, Slow, and Stop.
*   **Transform:** Casts Mini and Toad.
*   **Ultima:** Casts Ultima.

### Command Materia (Yellow)

*   **Deathblow:** Adds the "D.Blow" command.
*   **Double Cut:** Adds the "2x-Cut" and "4x-Cut" commands.
*   **Enemy Skill:** Allows the user to learn and use enemy abilities.
*   **Manipulate:** Adds the "Manip." command.
*   **Master Command:** Combines Deathblow, Manipulate, Mime, Morph, Sense, Steal, and Throw.
*   **Mime:** Adds the "Mime" command.
*   **Morph:** Adds the "Morph" command.
*   **Sense:** Adds the "Sense" command.
*   **Slash-All:** Adds the "Slash-All" and "Flash" commands.
*   **Steal:** Adds the "Steal" and "Mug" commands.
*   **Throw:** Adds the "Throw" and "Coin" commands.
*   **W-Item:** Allows the use of two items in one turn.
*   **W-Magic:** Allows the casting of two magic spells in one turn.
*   **W-Summon:** Allows the use of two summons in one turn.

### Independent Materia (Purple)

*   **Chocobo Lure:** Increases the chance of encountering Chocobos.
*   **Counter Attack:** Provides a chance to counterattack when hit.
*   **Cover:** Increases the chance of covering an ally from an attack.
*   **Enemy Away:** Decreases the random encounter rate.
*   **Enemy Lure:** Increases the random encounter rate.
*   **EXP Plus:** Increases experience points earned.
*   **Gil Plus:** Increases gil earned.
*   **HP <-> MP:** Swaps HP and MP values.
*   **HP Plus:** Increases maximum HP.
*   **Long Range:** Allows attacks to hit enemies in the back row.
*   **Luck Plus:** Increases the Luck stat.
*   **Magic Plus:** Increases the Magic stat.
*   **Mega All:** Allows spells to target all enemies or allies.
*   **MP Plus:** Increases maximum MP.
*   **Pre-Emptive:** Increases the chance of a pre-emptive strike.
*   **Speed Plus:** Increases the Speed stat.
*   **Underwater:** Removes the 20-minute time limit in the final dungeon.

### Support Materia (Blue)

*   **Added Cut:** Adds a physical attack after using the paired Materia.
*   **Added Effect:** Adds the status effect of the paired Materia to attacks or defense.
*   **All:** Allows the paired Materia's spell to target all enemies or allies.
*   **Counter:** Counters with the paired Materia's ability.
*   **Elemental:** Adds the element of the paired Materia to attacks or defense.
*   **Final Attack:** Uses the paired Materia right before the character is knocked out.
*   **HP Absorb:** Absorbs HP when using the paired Materia.
*   **Magic Counter:** Counters with the paired magic Materia.
*   **MP Absorb:** Absorbs MP when using the paired Materia.
*   **MP Turbo:** Increases the power of the paired Materia at the cost of more MP.
*   **Quadra Magic:** Allows the paired magic Materia to be cast four times in a row.
*   **Sneak Attack:** Provides a chance to start the battle by using the paired Materia.
*   **Steal as Well:** Steals from the enemy when using the paired Materia.

## Job System (Final Fantasy Tactics)

### Jobs

*   **Squire:** Basic skills.
*   **Chemist:** Item-based abilities.
*   **Knight:** Breaks enemy stats and equipment.
*   **Archer:** Charged attacks.
*   **Monk:** Hand-to-hand combat and healing.
*   **Priest (White Mage):** White magic.
*   **Wizard (Black Mage):** Black magic.
*   **Time Mage:** Time magic.
*   **Summoner:** Summon magic.
*   **Thief:** Stealing abilities.
*   **Orator (Mediator):** Talk-based abilities.
*   **Mystic (Oracle):** Yin-Yang magic.
*   **Geomancer:** Elemental terrain-based abilities.
*   **Lancer (Dragoon):** Jump abilities.
*   **Samurai:** Iaido abilities.
*   **Ninja:** Throwing abilities.
*   **Arithmetician (Calculator):** Math-based spellcasting.
*   **Bard:** Singing abilities (male only).
*   **Dancer:** Dancing abilities (female only).
*   **Mime:** Mimics party member actions.
